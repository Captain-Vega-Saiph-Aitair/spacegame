You are the captain of a starship. You have descovered an uninhabited and inhospitable planet deep in space.

Chapter 1 : Save up

Scout above the planet and find minerals to build up your capital. Destroy asteroids and collect the dust.

Chapter 2 : Establish your base

Build your spacestation. Explore planet and exploite planet. Sell off goods and manufacture more.

Chapter 3 : Secure supply route

With traffic coming and going this has becomes a target for bandets and any other unlawfuls. Expand your influence and pinpoint the opponents base of operations. Setup more ships now, what to do with the old ones...

Chapter 4 : Go on the Offensive

Travel to and attach the opponent.

Chapter 5 : The Great Beyond

Sandbox mode now, maybe go over other planets, mabye generate stronger enemies.

Attachments - Inserts on a ship for weapons or other tools. E.g. cannon, tractor beam or mining drill.

Targeting function - useful for player for auto fire and useful for the AI to determine closest opponent.

Auto Fire function  - a properity on a weapon that allows it to shot by itself. Auto fire is only interested if a player is using it.

Mining Industry

A very expensive endeavor right now.

  • Drilling - Destroy the asteriod to collect the rocks, metals and ice inside.
  • Size - Small as a meter up (1km, 7.8 x 10^12) to proto-planets the size of countries (230km, 2.5 x 10^19kg).
  • Yield - Average Earth mining: 0.0002% vs Average Space mining: 0.01% for useful metals
  • Materials - Water, Iron, Platimun.
  • Places - to find asteroids:
    • Astroid Belt - Between Inner and Outter planets.
    • Kuiper Belt - Outside the Outter planets .
    • Trojans - L-points in Outter planets
    • Other - Scatter anywhere
    • Oort Cloud - Outside the Solar System

Escalating Enemy

End of game sand-box modes.
  • Asteriod Bombarbment -  Natural waves come at you and they get harder. Enemy is throwing them at you and are getting better at it.
  • Armada - Enemy waves come at you and they get harder. Zerg idea, slowly grow, fast attackers, always sending bigger waves and growing
  • Black Hole Bomb - Two ingredients: 1. A fast spinning Blackhole, 2. Mirrors. Surround the Blackhole in mirrors. A controlled solar powerplant or can exploding as big as a supernova.
